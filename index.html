New Hebrew Reading section is up! ✨ December 24th, 2023

Additional new section dedicated to practice Hebrew reading is up. This page is designed to show you how the voweles A, E, I, O, U can appear in different texts and how to read them.

The idea is simple; you'll see sets of lettes with NIKUD, and you'll have a chance to read them in Hebrew and right below that check if you were correct. It starts from basic 1-2 letter sounds and goes all the way to words of 4-7 letters. At the end of the page you will find 3 exercises; those won't contain English translation and built to test the knowlege in our online classes .
