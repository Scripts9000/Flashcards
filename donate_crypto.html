Bitcoin is the currency of the Internet: a distributed, worldwide, decentralized digital money. Unlike any traditional currencies, Bitcoins are issued and managed without any central authority whatsoever: there is no government, company, or bank in charge of Bitcoin. As such, it is more resistant to inflation, regulations and banks. With Bitcoin, you can be your own bank.
