New Hebrew Reading section is up! ✨ December 24th, 2023

Additional new section dedicated to practice Hebrew reading is up. This page is designed to show you how the voweles A, E, I, O, U can appear in different texts and how to read them.

The idea is simple; you'll see sets of lettes with NIKUD, and you'll have a chance to read them in Hebrew and right below that check if you were correct. It starts from basic 1-2 letter sounds and goes all the way to words of 4-7 letters. At the end of the page you will find 3 exercises; those won't contain English translation and built to test the knowlege in our online classes .

To give it a try, navigate to the "Basics" link on the menu and select >> Read Hebrew. you'll find it all there; the sounds A, E, I, O, U, some practice material with English translation, and further down 3 levels of exercises Alternatively, you can click here for Read Hebrew

Practice section is up! ✨ November 12th, 2023

Just completed the addition of a new section dedicated to practicing Hebrew words from both Book 1 and Book 2 on our language study resource. This feature is designed to test your knowledge after engaging with the vocabulary section.

The concept is simple: you'll be presented with a single word, and you'll have four options to choose from. If you correctly identify the word, you earn a point; if not, your score decreases by two points. An additional benefit is the ability to practice each vocabulary set separately, enhancing your preparation for upcoming classes.

To give it a try, navigate to the "Practice" link on the menu and choose either Book 1 or Book 2. Within each book, you'll find all the units listed. Alternatively, you can click here for Book 1 or here for Book 2.
