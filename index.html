Latest Update!: ✔️ November 12th, 2023

✨Practice Section is up!✨

Just completed the addition of a new section dedicated to practicing Hebrew words from both Book 1 and Book 2 on our language study resource. This feature is designed to test your knowledge after engaging with the vocabulary section.

The concept is simple: you'll be presented with a single word, and you'll have four options to choose from. If you correctly identify the word, you earn a point; if not, your score decreases by two points. An additional benefit is the ability to practice each vocabulary set separately, enhancing your preparation for upcoming classes.

To give it a try, navigate to the "Practice" link on the menu and choose either Book 1 or Book 2. Within each book, you'll find all the units listed. Alternatively, you can click here for Book 1 or here for Book 2.
